Plate Recognition On-Demand (PROD)
Don't view hours of video materials-
just upload your files and we'll send you the results!
Limbio PROD is a free service for off-line recognition of license plates from video files at the user's request. This service provides automated non-periodic traffic measurement to help your company with business decisions.

Upload your file
for recognition at no cost

Upload your video file in one of these formats
(up to 2 GB): h264-encoded, AVI or MP4b.

Within 24 hours, you will receive an Excel file
listing the number plates recognized in your
video.

What Limbio PROD does

Counts the traffic passing by a particular location
where you plan to set up outdoor advertising.

Counts incoming / outgoing traffic at a particular
location, and record how long a particular vehicle
stayed at that location.

Measure any non-periodic traffic at any location you request.

Presents all the data in an Excel file.

What you can do with Limbio PROD does?

Stop viewing hours of video materials: you do the job you do best and leave the routine to the machines.

Make business decisions such as opening a new facility or entering a new region based on daily,weekly or monthly traffic in the surrounding area.

Measure the number of cars per day at your facilities / sites just by looking at our Excel report.

Measure traffic near outdoor advertisements to confirm their value for your customers, analyze the number of views, and justify the cost to advertisers.
